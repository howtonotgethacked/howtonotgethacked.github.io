🔍 How Phishing Works

  • Attackers create fake websites that look legitimate
  • They use similar URLs with slight misspellings
  • Victims enter their real credentials
  • Attackers steal and use these credentials
  • Often spread through email or social media

🛡️ How to Defend Against Phishing

  • Always check the URL carefully
  • Look for HTTPS and valid certificates
  • Never click links in suspicious emails
  • Use two-factor authentication
  • Type URLs directly into your browser
  • Use password managers
  • Be suspicious of urgent requests

🚨 Red Flags to Watch For

  • Misspelled domain names
  • Urgent security warnings
  • Requests for sensitive information
  • Poor grammar and spelling
  • Suspicious sender addresses
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers

📊 Phishing Statistics
